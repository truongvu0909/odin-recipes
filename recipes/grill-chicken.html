How to make grilled-chicken

This is a recipe on how to make grilled-chicken, please follow directions

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • oregano
  • chicken
  • lemon

Directions

  1. Transfer chicken to paper-towel-lined sheet pan to drain slightly.
  2. Whisk garlic, oregano, red pepper flakes, black pepper, lemon juice, olive oil, and vinegar together in a large bowl.
